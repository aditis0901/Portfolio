BUNNY-ROCKET GAME

Description:

This "Bunny-Rocket game" is developed using HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT.

The game starts with bunny at its initial position and game score as "0". as we overcome one trip of the rocket, the score accordingly increases. Once the bunny touches the rocket, OUCH! THE GAME's OVER!
